About me

My name is Alena
I'm a UX/UI web designer with more than 3 years of experience. I develop website design for small, medium and large businesses. I believe that the site should perform its tasks, be convenient and aesthetic. Therefore, when designing a design, I pay special attention to the structure and logic of interaction.

My task is to make sure that the client is satisfied. Also, I do not only develop website designs. But I also help novice designers, namely, I can view your site layout and point out errors in it! If you need it, write to me in any convenient network for you
